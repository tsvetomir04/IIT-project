The latest updates, patch notes, and announcements from across the galaxy.

ANOUNCEMENT May 1, 2026

Space Art Entertainment announces expansion of “Voidborn” universe

Space Art Entertainment has officially confirmed that the Voidborn: Genesis Protocol universe is expanding into a full narrative franchise. According to the developers, the goal is to turn Voidborn into a “living, evolving horror experience” where player decisions permanently affect future updates.

UPDATES May 7, 2026

Early concept visuals revealed for “Nebula Reign” galactic systems

The studio has released early concept art for Nebula Reign, showcasing massive empire-controlled sectors, dynamic nebula regions, and fully interactive galaxy maps. Development focus is currently on large-scale simulation systems where diplomacy, trade routes, and warfare constantly reshape the universe in real time.

RELEASE May 12, 2026

“Echoes of Orion” enters full narrative production phase

Development on Echoes of Orion has officially moved into full narrative production. The team is now focusing on environmental storytelling, voice design, and non-linear exploration systems across abandoned planetary zones. Players will experience a deeply atmospheric journey where each discovered signal slowly changes the understanding of the lost colony.

About Us

WHO WE ARE

We are Space Art Entertainment — a team of people who love making games and telling stories. We started with a simple idea: to create worlds that feel alive, where players can explore, discover, and get lost in something bigger than themselves.

Developers, artists and dreamers working together to turn imagination into playable experiences. We care about details, atmosphere, and making every moment in our games feel meaningful. For us, games are not just entertainment — they’re a way to share ideas, emotions, and stories with people around the world. And we are just getting started.
